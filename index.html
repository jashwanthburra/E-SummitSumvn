About Us



As young entrepreneurs driven by passion and a vision for our country's growth, we are thrilled to contribute meaningfully through initiatives that foster innovation and opportunity. With this enthusiasm, we are organizing an Entrepreneurship Summit that aims to bridge gaps and create a platform for all. Traditionally, such summits are exclusive to premier institutions like IITs and NITs, where students have the privilege of engaging with industry experts and trailblazing entrepreneurs. Unfortunately, many talented students from other engineering colleges remain unaware of or excluded from these transformative experiences. Our summit seeks to change this narrative by providing students from Level 2 engineering colleges with exposure to the entrepreneurial ecosystem. This event will facilitate dynamic interactions between students and seasoned entrepreneurs, sparking ideas that can evolve into groundbreaking products with societal impact. Moreover, we are committed to empowering women, instilling confidence, and fostering a belief in their immense potential. Through this initiative, we aim to inspire a generation of innovative thinkers and leaders who will drive meaningful change for a better tomorrow.
